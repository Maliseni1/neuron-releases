# Neuron — Your Living Lexicon 🧠

[![Version](https://img.shields.io/badge/version-1.0.0-blue.svg)](#) [![Platform](https://img.shields.io/badge/platform-Android%20%7C%20iOS-lightgrey.svg)](#)

**Neuron** is an intelligent, AI-powered dictionary and vocabulary curator. Built for logophiles and casual learners alike, Neuron transcends traditional dictionaries by utilizing generative AI to discover, define, and contextualize words you can't quite remember, and even transcribes your voice directly to search.

Built with ❤️ by **Chiza Labs**.

## ✨ Key Features

* **Generative AI Discovery:** Can't remember a word? Just describe it (e.g., *"the smell of rain"* or *"the fear of tight spaces"*), and Neuron's AI will find the exact word for you.
* **Voice-to-Text Search:** Native, frictionless audio transcription. Tap the mic, speak your thought, and let the AI instantly transcribe and search it.
* **Deep Context & Etymology:** Go beyond basic definitions. Neuron generates dynamic example sentences, synonyms, and fascinating etymologies for every word.
* **Your Personal Library:** Save newly discovered words directly to your device via an ultra-fast local SQLite database. Access your curated lexicon entirely offline.
* **Fluid UI/UX:** A minimalist, distraction-free interface featuring smart animations and dynamic layouts.

## 📥 Download & Installation

### Android (.apk)
1. Navigate to the [Releases](../../releases) tab on this repository.
2. Download the latest `Neuron-v1.0.0.apk` file to your Android device.
3. Open the file to install (you may need to allow "Install from Unknown Sources" in your settings).

### iOS
*Currently in private beta. iOS requires a TestFlight invitation due to Apple's sideloading restrictions.*

## 🔒 Privacy & Architecture
Neuron is built with privacy and efficiency in mind. All saved words are stored completely **locally** on your device. External AI requests are routed securely through the Chiza Labs serverless backend, ensuring your data is handled safely and efficiently.
